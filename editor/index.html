RPG Reactor v0.9.0

An open-source, cross-platform RPG game engine built with NW.js and PixiJS v8

Create amazing RPG games with a professional editor that runs on Windows, Mac, and Linux. Compatible with RPG Maker MZ and MV projects in most cases; runtime compatibility depends primarily on the project's corescripts and plugins.
